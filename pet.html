Adoption FAQs

What is the adoption fee included?

The standard fee covers complete veterinary checkups, first-round vaccinations, deworming microchipping, and spay/neuter surgery.

How long does the adoption process take?

Typically between 3 to 7 business days. This allows us to review your application and host a quick meet-and-greet sessions.
